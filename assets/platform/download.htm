Security, Privacy, & Compliance

Trust built into every dataset and definition.

Salesforce metadata links every object and dataset to its security, privacy, and compliance policies—so protection travels with your data wherever it’s used.

When data is stored in Salesforce, it inherits platform-level encryption, access controls, and audit capabilities.

When connected through Data 360, governance extends through Data 360’s Data Governance framework, ensuring lineage, consent, and policy enforcement across every system.

With metadata and data working together, every integration, workflow, and insight operates within the same trusted boundary.
