AI and the Future of Work: Whats Changing and How to Prepare

On-Demand Webinar

AI is transforming how work gets done - reshaping roles, redefining required skills, and challenging traditional workforce models. But staying competitive requires more than adopting the latest tools. It takes a clear strategy that balances automation with human expertise and prepares your teams for what's next.

Join Usman Nasir, VP, Agentforce Engineer, and Joahna Kuiper, RVP, Responsible AI Agentforce Engineer, as they break down what the rise of AI really means for your workforce - and how leading organizations are taking action.

In this session, you'll learn:

  • How AI is influencing the evolution of jobs, teams, and core business functions
  • Why striking the right balance between AI and human expertise is key
  • Practical steps to help your workforce adapt, upskill, and stay ahead

You'll leave with a clearer view of what's changing and how to lead your organization through it with confidence.
